Neptune

Neptune is the eighth and farthest planet from the Sun. It is a cold, dark, and windy ice giant with supersonic winds. Neptune has a deep blue color due to the absorption of red light by methane in its atmosphere.

Quick Facts

Distance from Sun

4.5 billion km (2.8 billion miles)

Orbital Period

165 Earth years

Moons

14 (largest is Triton)

Surface Temperature

-214°C (average)

Diameter

49,244 km

About Neptune

Neptune is the fourth-largest planet in diameter and the third-most-massive planet in the Solar System. Its dynamic atmosphere hosts the fastest winds recorded in the Solar System, reaching up to 2,100 km/h. Neptune's largest moon, Triton, is geologically active and orbits in the opposite direction of Neptune's rotation.
