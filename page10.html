About This Project

This website is a simple yet engaging educational project that explores the wonders of our Solar System. From planets and moons to their unique features, the goal is to present information in a visually interactive way that works well on both desktop and mobile.

Purpose

The purpose of this project is to provide a mobile-friendly, interactive way of learning about space. With animations, images, and responsive design, students and space enthusiasts alike can enjoy exploring our cosmic neighborhood.

Features

Interactive planet animations
Mobile-optimized navigation bar
Sections for planets, moons, and general information
A consistent theme with a cosmic background
Easy-to-expand structure for more content
